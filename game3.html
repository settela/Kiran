The prepartion for all of the three SKUs will remain same with the exception in last step where different flavours will be added (delayed differentiation). The startup will prioritise the highest revenue generating SKU for preparation and serving as in the order (DC_2 > DC_3 > DC_1: order of preference). The flavour addition will be done by one single machine only with different time taken for each SKUs. Also with the new process line setup, the entire prepration is done in four(4) steps. The performance will be measured on two parameters:
(a) Profit from selling: Revenue (Units Sold * Revenue per unit) - Expense (from renting of machine) &
(b) Service Level = Order Served / Incoming Orders.
For the service level > 95%, the user will be rewarded as [(service level-.95)* Revenue generated] in addition to (Revenue - Expense). Good Luck!!
